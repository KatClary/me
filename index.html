Hi, I'm Katherine Clary.

I'm a New York City based writer, independent publisher and editor.

My current work focuses on the intersection of viticulture, design and community.

My book Wine, Unfiltered (Hachette, 2020) discusses the culture, history and systems around natural winemaking and grape growing.
